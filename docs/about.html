I'm Pakistani, born and raised, now based in Tampa, Florida. AI grabbed my attention early and never really let go. Not the hype around it, but the actual, practical question of what it can do when you point it at a real problem.

CleoVoice is what came out of that. A set of tools built around the parts of work that eat up time without really needing a human there. Calls that need answering. Meetings that need follow-through. Interview prep that most people never get access to. I built tools for all of it.

I grew up seeing firsthand what a difference access to the right technology makes. Good tools are unevenly distributed, and I think AI is the best shot we've had in a long time to fix that. That's the thing that keeps me building.

Mission
Why CleoVoice exists

Most of the people who could benefit most from AI are also the furthest from it. Small business owners running on thin margins, professionals who never got proper coaching, teams where everyone's too busy to follow up on anything. That's who I build for.

Every CleoVoice product is built around one simple idea: find the thing that's eating someone's time, and make it disappear. Not with a platform that takes months to configure, but with something that just works. Cleo handles calls. Echo handles meetings. MockMind handles interview prep. Each one does its job and stays out of the way.

I want to build things that are genuinely useful to people who never expected to have access to tools like this. That's the mission. Everything else follows from it.
