Under the hood

Built on best-in-class AI infrastructure

Echo combines the most accurate speech model available with frontier AI reasoning to turn raw audio into structured intelligence.

Deepgram (STT)

Nova-2 speech-to-text with speaker diarisation. Handles accents, crosstalk, and low-quality audio with industry-leading accuracy.

Claude Sonnet (AI analysis)

Reads the full transcript to generate summaries, extract action items with owners and dates, and surface key decisions in structured form.

PostgreSQL (storage)

All transcripts, summaries, and action items stored securely in a relational database you control — searchable and available forever.

React + FastAPI (interface)

A fast, clean React frontend paired with a FastAPI backend — upload files, view results, search history, and export from one place.

FAQ

Common questions

What audio formats does Echo support? +
MP3, MP4, WAV, M4A, and most common audio and video formats. Maximum file size is 500MB. If you have a larger file, export the audio track separately before uploading.
How accurate is the transcription? +
Typically 95%+ on clear audio. Deepgram Nova-2 is optimised for meetings and handles multiple speakers well. Accuracy may vary on very noisy audio or heavy accents, but Echo still produces a highly usable transcript in those cases.
Is my meeting data private? +
Yes. Your recordings and transcripts are stored in databases we control and are never used to train AI models. Only you have access to your meeting data.
Can I use Echo for video calls? +
Yes. Export the audio from Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet and upload it directly to Echo. Most video call platforms let you download a local recording — just grab that file and you're good to go.
