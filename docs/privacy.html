Overview

CleoVoice ("we", "us", "our") operates Cleo, Echo, MockMind, and this website. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have over it.

We collect only what we need to deliver our services. We do not sell your data. We do not use your data to train AI models without your explicit consent.

What we collect

Cleo — AI Voice Receptionist

  • Caller phone numbers and names (provided during calls)
  • Call audio recordings and transcriptions (processed via Twilio and stored temporarily)
  • Appointment details: service address, requested service type, preferred times
  • Call metadata: duration, timestamp, outcome (booked / not booked)
  • Business account information: company name, phone number, integration credentials

Echo — AI Meeting Intelligence

  • Audio or video files you upload for transcription
  • Transcripts, AI summaries, and extracted action items generated from your recordings
  • Speaker labels and names you assign
  • Account information: name, email address, password hash

MockMind — AI Interview Coach

  • Resume files you upload (stored and used only to generate questions for your sessions)
  • Your written or voice-transcribed answers during interview sessions
  • Session data: scores, feedback, session history, performance reports
  • Account information: name, email address, password hash

This website

  • Name and email address submitted via contact or demo request forms
  • Basic analytics: page views, referral source, browser type (no personal identifiers)

How we use your data

  • To deliver the service you signed up for
  • To sync appointment and call data to your connected tools (HubSpot, Jobber, Google Calendar)
  • To send transactional communications (booking confirmations, summaries, account alerts)
  • To respond to demo requests and support inquiries
  • To improve product reliability and debug issues
We do not use your data to train AI models. Transcripts, call recordings, interview answers, and meeting content are never used to train third-party or first-party AI systems without your explicit written consent.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate our products. Each has its own privacy policy.

  • Twilio — call routing and SMS for Cleo. Processes call audio and phone numbers.
  • Deepgram — speech-to-text transcription for Cleo and Echo. Processes audio content.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI processing for summaries, analysis, and interview coaching. Content is processed per Anthropic's API terms.
  • HubSpot / Jobber / Google Calendar — optional integrations. Data is shared only if you connect these tools and only the data needed to sync appointments or contacts.
  • PostgreSQL (self-hosted) — your data is stored in databases we control, not handed to a third-party storage provider.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for data processing where consent is the basis
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority in your jurisdiction

To exercise any of these rights, email us at jahanzeb2005@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Data retention

  • Cleo call recordings: retained for 90 days, then permanently deleted unless you request earlier deletion
  • Call transcripts and summaries: retained for the lifetime of your account
  • Echo recordings and transcripts: retained for the lifetime of your account
  • MockMind sessions and reports: retained for the lifetime of your account
  • Account data: deleted within 30 days of account closure upon request

Cookies

This website uses essential cookies only (session management, preference storage). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. We do not use third-party analytics services that track you across other websites.

Children

Our services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has submitted data to us, contact us and we will delete it promptly.
