How it works

From call to booked job in under 2 minutes

1

Customer calls your number

Cleo picks up instantly — no hold music, no voicemail. It greets them by name if they've called before and gets straight to the point.

2

Cleo collects all the job details

Problem description, service address, preferred date, and name — all confirmed and verified before booking. Cleo even reads the address back to catch mistakes.

3

Appointment booked, everything synced

The job lands in your Google Calendar, a deal is created in HubSpot or Jobber, and you get an email summary — all within seconds of the call ending.
