How it works

From ring to booked job in under 2 minutes

1

Customer calls your number

Cleo picks up instantly — no hold music, no voicemail. It greets returning customers by name and asks how it can help.

2

Cleo collects every detail

Problem description, service address, preferred date, full name — confirmed and read back to prevent errors. Cleo even checks if the address is in your service area.

3

Everything syncs automatically

Job lands in your Google Calendar. Contact created in HubSpot or Jobber. Summary email arrives in your inbox within seconds. Nothing slips through.

Returning Caller Recognition

After-Hours Handling
