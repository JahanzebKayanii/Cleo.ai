FAQ

Common questions

How does Cleo know about my business? +
During setup, you upload your business documents — services list, pricing, FAQs, service area, policies. These get embedded into a knowledge base Cleo searches on every call. When your pricing changes, you update the document and Cleo instantly knows. Nothing is hardcoded.
What if Cleo can't answer a question? +
If a question isn't covered in your knowledge base, Cleo says it will have a team member follow up — it never makes up an answer. If the caller is frustrated or wants a human, Cleo transfers them to your real phone number immediately.
Does it work with my existing phone number? +
Yes. We provision a dedicated phone number for Cleo and forward your existing business number to it. Your customers dial the same number they always have — nothing changes on their end.
How long does setup take? +
Usually under 24 hours. We need your services list, business hours, service area, and calendar access. Most of it is a 30-minute conversation and a few documents. We handle all the technical setup.
What happens to my call data? +
Every call is transcribed, summarised, and stored in your private dashboard. Only you have access. We never share or sell call data. Transcripts and recordings are available to you indefinitely.
Can Cleo handle after-hours calls? +
Yes. Cleo runs 24/7. Outside your configured business hours, it lets callers know you're closed, collects their name and details, and sends you a summary so you can call back first thing in the morning. No lead is ever lost to voicemail.
Can I change the business hours or services without contacting you? +
Yes. Your dashboard has a configuration panel where you can update business hours, service area, services offered, and integration settings yourself — no technical skills required. Changes go live immediately.
What if I want to cancel? +
Cancel anytime with no penalty. Your call history and data are exported to you before we close anything. We don't lock you into contracts.
