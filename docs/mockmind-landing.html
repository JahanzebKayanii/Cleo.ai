FAQ

Common questions

What interview formats does MockMind support? +
Behavioral, technical, coding (LeetCode-style), and mixed. You can also specify the company for company-specific prep — MockMind tailors its questions to what that employer is known for asking.
Can I practice with my resume? +
Yes. Upload your CV and MockMind references your actual experience when generating questions and evaluating how well your answers match your background. It notices when you're underselling a strong project — and says so.
Does voice input really work? +
Yes. MockMind uses Deepgram to transcribe your spoken answers in real time and evaluates them the same way as typed answers. Speaking out loud trains the muscle memory you need for the actual interview room.
How is my score calculated? +
Claude evaluates your answer against best-practice frameworks — STAR for behavioral questions, technical depth and trade-off reasoning for engineering questions — and outputs a 1–10 score with specific commentary on exactly what was strong, what was missing, and how to fix it.
