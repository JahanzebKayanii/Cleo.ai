Company Overview

Apex Home Services is a licensed and insured home services company based in Austin, Texas. We have been serving residential and commercial customers since 2008. Our team of certified technicians specializes in HVAC, plumbing, and electrical services. We are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week for emergency calls.

Warranty and Guarantees

All labor is covered by our 1-year workmanship warranty. Equipment warranties vary by manufacturer, typically 5 to 10 years for major HVAC units and water heaters. We guarantee our work 100% and will return at no charge if the issue recurs within the warranty period.

Appointment Booking

Customers can book appointments by calling our main line at (512) 555-0198 or through our website. We offer same-day appointments for urgent issues subject to availability. Standard appointments are booked in 2-hour arrival windows (8am-10am, 10am-12pm, 12pm-2pm, 2pm-4pm, 4pm-6pm).

Payment Methods

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), check, and offer financing through Greensky with approved credit. Payment is due at time of service for repairs. A 50% deposit is required for installations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you offer free estimates?
A: We offer free estimates for new installations and replacements. Diagnostic fees apply for repair calls but are waived if you proceed with the repair.

Q: How quickly can you respond to an emergency?
A: For emergency calls, we aim to have a technician on-site within 2 hours. During peak demand periods this may extend to 3 to 4 hours.

Q: Are your technicians background checked?
A: Yes, all Apex technicians pass a full background check and drug screening before joining our team. We are fully licensed, bonded, and insured.

Q: Do you offer service contracts or maintenance plans?
A: Yes, our Apex Care Plan covers annual tune-ups for HVAC and water heater, priority scheduling, and a 15% discount on repairs. The plan costs $199 per year for a single system or $299 for whole-home coverage.

Q: What brands of equipment do you carry?
A: We primarily install Carrier and Trane for HVAC, Rheem for water heaters, and Generac for generators. We can also source other brands on request.

Q: Can I get a second opinion if I already have a quote?
A: Absolutely. We are happy to review any existing quote and provide our own assessment. The diagnostic fee applies but will be credited toward the repair if you choose us.
